The Oakridge Fencing Club is a MeetUp group that is passionate about the art of swordsmanship. Come join our vibrant community today! ALL experience levels welcome!

There three types of fencing available

  • For those who are just getting into fencing, we recommend foil
  • Looking for a style that is more reminiscent of historical swordplay, then Epee fencing if for you
  • For something fast pace and physically demanding, we've got you covered with Saber
